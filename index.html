About Me

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG), Chennai — one of India's most respected engineering institutions. My work sits at the crossroads of mechanical design, automation, and sustainable innovation.

I love taking an idea from napkin sketch to functional prototype. Whether it's a colour-sensing robotic arm, a biodegradable packaging solution, or a hybrid IC engine — I'm driven by the challenge of making things that actually work.
